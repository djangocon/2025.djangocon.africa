First DjangoCon Africa

In 2023 we hosted the first ever DjangoCon Africa. DjangoCon Africa took place in Zanzibar, Tanzania 6th-11th November 2023.

We had roughly 200 attendees, of whom 103 were women. The attendees represented at least 22 countries from around the world.

We held 26 talks on topics covering programming, society, careers, education, disability, agriculture - the full range of topics youâ€™d expect at a DjangoCon, with the African perspective often at the forefront.
